Why Van Dusen Capital LLC

Financial advice is often fragmented. One source focuses on investing, another focuses on insurance, and another talks about saving. Rarely do these conversations come together into a complete strategy. Van Dusen Capital is focused on connecting those pieces so you can understand how protection, growth, and tax efficiency work together.

Many people are unaware of how certain strategies, such as max funded indexed universal life, can fit into a broader financial plan. When structured correctly, these strategies may offer flexibility, long-term growth potential, and tax advantages. However, they are often misunderstood because they are not explained clearly. This site is designed to change that.

Life insurance with living benefits is another area that has evolved significantly. Instead of being something that only pays out after death, certain policies may provide access to funds during serious health events. Understanding how this works can change the way you think about protection entirely.
