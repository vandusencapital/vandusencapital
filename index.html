What Is a Max Funded IUL?

A max funded Indexed Universal Life policy is a strategy used by many people who want tax advantaged growth, access to policy loans, and life insurance protection in one structure. When designed properly, a max funded IUL can support tax free retirement income potential while helping reduce exposure to market losses.

Why Living Benefits Matter

Life insurance with living benefits can provide meaningful flexibility by allowing access to benefits during qualifying critical, chronic, or terminal health events. This can help protect savings, preserve income, and provide support when families need it most.

Business Insurance for Owners and Entrepreneurs

Business insurance is a core part of long-term protection planning. Van Dusen Capital helps business owners review solutions that may include liability protection, property coverage, and other customized options based on the business type and risk profile.
